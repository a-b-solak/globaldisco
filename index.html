Bias beyond Borders:
Global Inequalities in AI-Generated Music

This page consists of audio samples that demonstrate the biases found in our paper "Bias beyond Borders: Global Inequalities in AI-Generated Music". We present four challenging regional genres using examples from three music generation models, Suno, Mureka, and Riffusion, which performed comparatively worse on niche musical styles and generated songs that are perceptually closer to tracks from other (often more mainstream) genres.

Each row consists of the following components:

  • Prompt:The musical style prompt from the GlobalDISCO dataset that was used as input to the model.
  • Reference: A YouTube link of a real-world track from the artist thos musical style the prompt describes. This serves as a ground truth example.
  • Generated: The audio output from the AI model based on the prompt.
  • Nearest Neighbor: A YouTube link of the closest mainstream genre (pop or rock) track, calculated and aggregated across multiple embedding spaces.
